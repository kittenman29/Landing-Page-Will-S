Big Sky, MT

This hike is great for people of all experience levels. There are some steep parts near the summit, but the panoramic views near the top are well worth the work to get there. Caution: High Altitude

Abby Thompson

Banff, AB

Apr 4, 2016

This trail starts at a gnarly 13,000 feet and is for expert level climbers only. There are parts near the middle where you will need either a guide or proper rapelling gear to traverse the rock face. There is a camping spot near the summit for people who have no brain, I would not recommend staying there as the weather can turn nasty in a quick hurry in those locations.

Secluded Beach Paradise

Will Schulz

Luquillo, PR

December 14, 2018

This hike is about a 30 minute one way trip from the car to the secluded beach. Located just past the Conquistador Resort, it is a trek through the jungle to get to the pristine sands. Because it is remote, there usually aren't too many people out there. It's located in a spot where there are minimal waves and it's perfect for relaxatioin.

August 28, 2016
